The chapter includes a quantitative-style example about U.S. use of force and relative power gap. The recognizable point is that statistical association can show a patterned relation, but association alone does not settle causality.

### NATO and Warsaw Pact trajectories

The chapter uses the different fate of NATO and the Warsaw Pact to illustrate causal mechanism and comparative process reasoning. A mechanism is not merely that events occurred in sequence; it explains how institutional or reconciliation processes operated differently.

Use for:
